Accounting is the process of recording financial transactions pertaining to a business. The accounting process includes summarizing, analyzing, and reporting these transactions to oversight agencies, regulators, and tax collection entities. The financial statements used in accounting are a concise summary of financial transactions over an accounting period, summarizing a company's operations, financial position, and cash flows. Accounting is one of the key functions of almost any business. It may be handled by a bookkeeper or an accountant at a small firm. The reports generated by various streams of accounting, such as cost accounting and managerial accounting, are invaluable in helping management make informed business decisions.

Dashboards are digital interfaces that group and organize information. They are central locations for data and insights about a specific topic. In a webinar platform, the webinar dashboard gives an overview of information about a webinar. Benefits of webinar dashboards
Benefits of webinar dashboards Webinar dashboards make data transparent, save time, and improve decision making. With a webinar dashboard, organizers have direct access to data. They can monitor if metrics are being met and easily build reports with the information they find. It is also easier to analyze data when it is all grouped in one place. Quick insights can be gained from easy to understand visuals. And unbiased data can hold teams accountable to their KPIs.
